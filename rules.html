Game Rules:

🏀 One team will play at a time.

🏀 Teams will use a men's ball unless the team consists of two females then a women's ball will be used.

🏀 One player will begin with the ball at half court, while the other may begin at any point within the half court playing area

🏀 Play will being when the court supervisor blows the whistle to begin the clock, the player at half court must pass the ball into the player in the front court who will then dribble or move to which ever spot they want

🏀Each team will have 1 minute to scorea as many points as possible

Shooting Rules

🏀 Players must start with at least one foot on the shooting area.

🏀 Each team may shoot a maximum of two layups

🏀 Players can not shoot from the same spot consecutively (back to back)

🏀 Players can not shoot the ball consecutively (back to back), shots must be alternated between teammates (take turns)

🏀 Players must attempt to shoot from all spots on the floor

Violations

🏀 Players must pass or dribble between shooting spots, one point will be deducted for each basketball moving violation (traveling, double dribble).

🏀 No player may take consecutive shots, shots must alternate between teammates. All invalid shots will not be counted toward final score.

🏀 Players can not shoot from the same spot consecutively, shots taken from the same spot consecutively will not be counted

🏀 Teams that do not shoot from each spot will result in a deduction of 5 points from the total score

🏀 Time will only stop if the ball becomes stuck in between the backboard and the rim or any other place near the playing area. If this occurs the time will be stopped and play will continue wit the last plaer to touch the ball standin gin the spot at which he /she shot it, and their teammate inbounding the ball under the basket.
